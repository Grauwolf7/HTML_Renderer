The Secret Connection Between Frogs and Zelda

Welcome to this Unique Adventure

Have you ever wondered why Tears of the Kingdom has so many frogs? Let's hop into this adventure!

  1. Frogs in Zelda Games

    • Hot-Footed Frogs boost Link's stamina
    • Tireless Frogs make perfect elixirs

    • Where to Find These Magical Amphibians

      • Lakes of Hyrule
      • Swamps near Kakariko Village
      • Hidden spots near waterfalls

  2. Why Frogs Are Simply Amazing

    • They go 'ribbit' (universal frog language!)
    • They can eat their weight in insects
    • Some change colors like a Chameleon armor set
